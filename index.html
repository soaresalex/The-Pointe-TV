About The Pointe
A rebellious teenager, Krish Shetty, is sent to work as a lifeguard in an exclusive beach town community, Pointe May. Krish quickly finds himself in trouble on the first day at the Pointe, when he takes an interest in one of the most popular girls in town, Sam Blake. Sam is also the object of affection for the town's golden boy and Krish’s boss, Jason Crouse. After sparking a friendship with Sam’s loner younger brother, Ryan, Krish convinces Ryan to help him crash Jason’s summer swing kickoff party. A stunt crafted to impress Sam. The Pointe follows the lives of these four individuals in a town that is seemingly unaffected by the problems of the outside world. New friendships will be made, old ones will be tested. Chaos will surely ensue in this comedy of errors from the minds of the creators CJ Morsey and Vishnu Prasad.
