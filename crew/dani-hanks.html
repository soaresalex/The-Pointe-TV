Dani Hanks

Executive Producer

I’m Dani Hanks and I’m a Director/Writer/Producer based in New York City. Since graduating from NYU Tisch Drama in 2015, I have written and directed a handful of short films, as well as music videos in American Sign Language. I have been involved in production on short-form projects, pilots, and indie films. I’m incredibly excited to be producing The Pointe, and is proud to contribute to such a quirky script and work with such a fun team!
