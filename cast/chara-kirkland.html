I’m Chara and I’m from Pennsylvania. I moved to NYC three years ago where I studied at The American Musical and Dramtic Academy (AMDA). I have been working as a actress singer and dancer primarily in Musical Theatre. My recent work include "Gypsy" at The Gallery Players and "La Cage Aux Folles" at The Axelrod PAC. I’m excited to have my first on screen debut with "The Pointe" and I’m looking forward to this brand new adventure.
