Hey guys! My name is Amanda Crown and I'm so excited to be a part of this awesome production. I'm originally from NYC and recently graduated from Vanderbilt University with a BA in Performance and Mass Media Studies. A few months after graduating I moved out to LA and have been pursing acting from the west coast ever since! I've studied at Strasberg, Adler, and Stonestreet Studios at NYU and am currently studying at Lesly Kahn in LA. You can catch me in a couple of web series, some independent films, and as the face of June Jacobs Spa Collection. So psyched to be joining this amazing project!
