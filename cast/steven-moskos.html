Steven Moskos

Garrett

Steven Moskos rose to fame playing the character Ellis in the Off Broadway musical adaptation of Die Hard. His ballad Hans Bubby (I'm Your White Knight) went silver in Australia. Since then he's become an MFA Acting graduate from Columbia University. Now he embarks on a new mission (he, being me. I'm Steven Moskos... Was that not clear?). He--as in me--is to portray the role of Garrett, the alcoholic, failed magician, for the silver screen. Boy can he--as in me--make a beer disapbeer. Disappear! Forgive me, for I, as in Steven Moskos, am a method actor, and I have been preparing most of the night... and day. Who am i again?
